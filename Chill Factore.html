Chill Factore

Who needs the Alps when you've got real snow in Manchester?
Chill Factore is the UK's longest indorr ski slope - and it's freezing in the best way possible.
Whether you're snowboarding like a pro, trying skiing for the first time or just falling over in style on a donut ring, this place is pure chaos and fun (in a good way).
You don't even need your own gear - they have all the gear you need to get going. Just bring gloves and, you know, a strong sense of balance.

The Science and Industry Museum

Ready to get all geeked up in the most awesome way?
The Science and Industry Museum is filled with mind-bending inventions, gaming-grade gadgets, and legendary machines that transformed the planet.
In the centre of Manchester, here you'll find a blend of history and future technology straight out of the movies.
From steam engines to space technology and, yes, things you can put your hands on, they've got it all.

📍Location: Liverpool Road, Manchester M3 4FP
🕒Open Daily: 10am - 5pm
🎫Entry: Free (yep, totally free)

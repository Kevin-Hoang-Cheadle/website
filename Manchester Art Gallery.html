Manchester Art Gallery

Think art galleries are boring? Not so fast.
The Manchester Art Gallery has it all ranging from classic paintings to weirdly great modern works that'll make you stop and go, "Wait... what?".
Whether you prefer striking colours, emotional vibes or just looking for an aesthetic background for your next Insta pic, the place delivers.

📍 Location: Mosley Street, Manchester M2 3JL
🕒 Open: Monday–Sunday, 10 AM – 5 PM
🎫 Entry: Free (because being cultured shouldn't cost a fortune)
